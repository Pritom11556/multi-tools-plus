About Case Converter

This tool helps you transform text case quickly and easily. Perfect for formatting content, adjusting text for different purposes, or fixing case issues in your writing.

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all characters to capital letters
  • lowercase: Converts all characters to small letters
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence
  • Capitalize Words: Capitalizes the first letter of each word
  • aLtErNaTiNg: Alternates between upper and lowercase letters
  • iNVERSE cASE: Inverts the current case of each character
